About

Growing up I always wanted to escape the rat race, and live simply. I ended up living that dream in Fairplay, CO when I was 28 with my then husband and 1.5 year old daughter. Little did I know life had other plans for me which, of course, I am only coming to see now. I was pregnant about to take leave for my second child when I found out my husband was having an affair. Needless to say, I took that pretty hard and lost all self esteem and will to go on. However after some time, I pushed through and I resdiscovered my love for computers and the internet through a coding course. A lot of time finding myself and believing in myself has gotten me to where I am today, someone filled with excitement for the future.
